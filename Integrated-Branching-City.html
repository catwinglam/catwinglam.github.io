Integrated Branching City
interactive video installation / Computational Art, 2019

Integrated Branching City is an interactive installation which aims to raise the awareness on the instability in Hong Kong. This work collects the participants’ data of attention in electroencephalography (EEG) and forms a new city silhouette and city symphony.
People faced instability and stress in Hong Kong. In a metaphor, the situation of the instable psychological state of many Hong Kong people is like that of the urban diseases’ patients. As the EEG data collected is combined with the simulated data of urban diseases’ patients, the possibility of social unity will be revealed by the participants.


Collaborated with Janet Choi, Ting Kwok and Bobo Ngai
