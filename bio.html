Biography catwinglam@gmail.com
Wandering around, but still makes art intermittently, works comprise electronics, video, sound installations and programming art.
Daily creations are self-made edgy musical instruments and installations.

到處飄泊的自由工作者，仍斷斷續續地創作，作品涉及電子、影像、聲音裝置、程式裝置等，日常練習為自製的古靈精怪樂器及裝置。
